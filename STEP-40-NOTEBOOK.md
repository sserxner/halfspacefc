# Step 40 — Notebook

## Purpose

Notebook is the private writing and research workspace inside Half Space Studio.
Nothing becomes public merely because it was written or saved here.

## Included

- Full-page writing with automatic private saving
- Multiple pages, search, tags, pinning, duplication, archiving, and deletion
- Manual saved versions with a 30-version recovery history
- Reusable Media Manager images and saved Tactics Board embeds
- Deliberate copying into a Matchday Diary or Transfer Recommendation
- The original Notebook page remains intact after conversion

## Publishing boundary

Notebook does not silently create a public post. A copied draft must still be
reviewed and saved through the destination editorial composer, then included in
the normal Publish Changes review. A dedicated public Notebook navigation tab
can be introduced during the visual/navigation redesign if the owner chooses.
