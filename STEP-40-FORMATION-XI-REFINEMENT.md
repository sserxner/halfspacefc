# Step 40 — Formation and XI Refinement

- Every enabled formation created in Settings automatically appears in the
  Tactics Board under **Site formations**.
- Global XI pitch coordinates are reused when that formation opens on the
  Tactics Board.
- Saved tactical scenarios remain separate because they may contain custom
  attacking and defending shapes rather than a standard XI.
- Club and Country reader pools automatically include ranked players when the
  team appears in the player’s ranking details.
- Ranking sections provide broad eligible positions; manual **Set reader
  players** entries add exceptions or override a player’s positions.
- XI builders share refined pitch markings, subtle empty-position markers,
  prominent serif player names, cleaner selectors, benches, and controls.
