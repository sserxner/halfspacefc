# Step 40 — Tactics and Editorial Refinement

- Tactics boards now use a portrait pitch matching their vertical attacking axis.
- Full, half, final-third, middle-third, and defensive-third views draw their
  actual pitch areas without restricting free player dragging.
- Portrait geometry is shared by editing, saved drafts, embeds, and PNG export.
- Diary and Transfer composers accept Media Manager images, memes, HTTPS video
  embeds, and saved tactics boards.
- Media supports captions, credits, accessible text, size, alignment, and
  before/after-writing placement.
- Boards support size, alignment, placement, and reopening of the editable
  original.
- Live preview can switch between desktop and phone widths.
