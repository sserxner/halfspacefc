# Step 40 — Verified Autofill Expansion

Purpose: prepare factual player-card data at the moment it is needed while keeping the owner in control.

- Every ranked player now offers “Prepare verified autofill.”
- The preparer uses the player’s Wikipedia page and linked Wikidata record.
- It prepares available date of birth, nationality, current club, career span, club league appearances/goals, team honours by stint, and individual honours.
- Assists and any field that cannot be read consistently remain blank.
- The source, preparation date, limitations, and review warnings stay attached to the draft.
- Preparing or loading never saves or publishes. The owner must review the populated form and deliberately save it.
- The original Messi, Ronaldo, and Neuer reviewed pilots remain available immediately.

The Tactics Board pitch markings now use the same vertical orientation as its saved player, arrow, label, and zone coordinates. Existing coordinates do not need to move.
