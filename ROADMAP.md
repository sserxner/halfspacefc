# Half Space product roadmap

These ideas are preserved for later product steps; they are not claims about
features already available.

## Editorial and media

- A Notebook area, informed by the earlier Transfer Recommendations concept
- Inline media for Matchday Diaries, Transfer Recommendations, and Notebook:
  reusable images, memes, video embeds, and photographs
- Tactics-board widget embedded within posts

## Player cards

Current-player cards should support optional, owner-entered fields:

- transfer value
- player comparisons
- current team
- age
- next move / clubs that should be interested
- suggested move

Any blank optional field must be completely omitted from the published card.

## Design and experience

- Full visual/style decisions after structural work is stable
- Refine Settings and publishing layouts, including checkbox proportions,
  spacing, hierarchy, and overall polish
- Continue monitoring Club/Country XI opening and Back-button performance

## XIs

- Continue formation-aware player options
- Preserve compatible selections when formations change
- Exclude a player from dropdowns only while selected in the XI or bench
- Prevent duplicate XI/bench assignments
